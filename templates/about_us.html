About Us

Welcome to Glider Flight Planner, the brainchild of a passionate developer and glider pilot dedicated to enhancing the safety and experience of the gliding community.

Our Story

Flying through the skies, feeling the rush of the wind, and experiencing the world from a bird's-eye view is an unparalleled experience. But with great adventure comes great responsibility. As a glider pilot, I've always felt the need for tools that can make our flights safer and more predictable. This led me to combine my skills as a developer with my passion for gliding, resulting in the creation of Glider Flight Planner.

Our Mission

At Glider Flight Planner, our mission is simple: to provide glider pilots with state-of-the-art tools that ensure safety without compromising the thrill of the flight. We believe that with the right tools and information, every flight can be both safe and exhilarating.

Why Glider Flight Planner?

  • Designed by a Pilot: Understanding the nuances and intricacies of gliding is crucial. Being a glider pilot myself, I've ensured that every feature of Glider Flight Planner is tailored to meet the real-world needs of our community.
  • Safety First: Our primary goal is to enhance safety. Every feature, every line of code is written with the safety of our pilots in mind.
  • Community Driven: We believe in the power of community. Glider Flight Planner is not just an app; it's a platform where glider pilots from around the world can come together, share, and learn.

Join Us on This Journey

Whether you're a seasoned glider pilot or just starting out, Glider Flight Planner is here to assist you on every flight. We invite you to explore our app, provide feedback, and be a part of our growing community. Together, let's make the skies safer and our adventures more memorable.
