Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Glider Flight Planner?

Answer: Glider Flight Planner is a state-of-the-art flight planning application designed specifically for the gliding community. It provides tools and features that help glider pilots plan their flights with safety and precision.

2. How do I get started with Glider Flight Planner?

Answer: You can start planning your flights immediately. Our user-friendly interface ensures a smooth experience even for first-time users.

3. Is Glider Flight Planner free to use?

Answer: Yes, Glider Flight Planner is free to use. However, you can support the development by buying me a coffee!

4. How does the Dynamic Form Field work?

Answer: The Dynamic Form Field allows users to manually enter multiple center locations for their flights. You can add as many locations as you need by clicking on the plus sign.

5. I encountered an error while using the app. What should I do?

Answer: We apologize for the inconvenience. Please contact us with details of the error, and our team will address it promptly.

6. How do I provide feedback or suggestions?

Answer: We value your feedback and suggestions. Please visit our contact page to share your thoughts or report any issues.

7. Is my data secure with Glider Flight Planner?

Answer: Absolutely. We prioritize user data security and have implemented robust measures to protect your information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

8. Can I use Glider Flight Planner on mobile devices?

Answer: Yes, Glider Flight Planner is designed to be responsive and works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.

9. How often is the app updated?

Answer: We continuously strive to improve Glider Flight Planner and regularly roll out updates to enhance performance, fix bugs, and introduce new features.

10. I have more questions. How can I reach out?

Answer: We're here to help! Please visit our contact page to get in touch with our support team.
