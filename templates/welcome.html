Welcome to the Glider Flight Planner!

This application allows you to plan your glider flights by selecting landing locations and inputting flight parameters and wind conditions.

The resulting high resolution map shows minimum altitude countour rings based on the selected flight parameters. You can overlay the countour rings on top of satellite imagery, topographic maps and FAA sectional charts.

This tool is intended solely as a ground planning tool. Use it as part of your pre-flight.

If you'd like to have a landing location added to the list of available locations, please contact us with the location details.

To get started, please read the User Guide and Disclaimer.
